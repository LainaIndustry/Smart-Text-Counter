Frequently Asked Questions

Are your tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use. We don't have premium versions or paid features. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! You can use all our tools immediately without any registration. We respect your privacy and want to make our tools as accessible as possible.

Is my text stored on your servers?

No. Our tools process text directly in your browser. We don't store or transmit your text to our servers, ensuring complete privacy for your documents.

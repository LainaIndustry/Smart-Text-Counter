About Smart Text Counter

Learn about our mission to provide free, powerful text analysis tools for everyone.

Our Story

Smart Text Counter was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to provide writers, students, and professionals with free, accessible tools that help them analyze and improve their writing. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful text analysis tools without barriers or costs.

What started as a basic word counter has evolved into a comprehensive suite of text analysis tools designed to meet the diverse needs of our growing community of users.

Our Mission

We're committed to:

  • Providing free, high-quality text analysis tools
  • Continuously improving our tools based on user feedback
  • Maintaining user privacy and data security
  • Supporting writers and content creators at all levels
  • Making text analysis accessible to everyone

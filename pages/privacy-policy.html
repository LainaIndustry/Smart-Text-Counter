Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to Smart Text Counter. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

Text You Process

We want to be completely transparent: we do not store, save, or transmit the text you process through our tools. All text analysis and processing happens directly in your browser. Your documents remain completely private and never leave your device.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit our site:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, tools used
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes
  • Cookies: To improve your experience and analyze site traffic

How We Use Your Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Improve our tools and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure website security
  • Provide and maintain our services

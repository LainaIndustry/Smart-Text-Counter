What is Smart Text Counter?

Smart Text Counter is a comprehensive online text analysis tool designed for writers, students, bloggers, and professionals. Our platform provides real-time statistics and insights about your text to help improve writing quality, meet word count requirements, and optimize content for various purposes.

Real-Time Analysis

Get instant feedback on word count, character count, reading time, and more as you type.

Multiple Tools

Access a suite of text manipulation tools including case conversion, list sorting, and text cleaning.

Writing Insights

Understand your writing better with detailed statistics about sentence structure and word usage.

Free & Accessible

All our tools are completely free to use with no registration required.

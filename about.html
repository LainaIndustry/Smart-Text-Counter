About Smart Text Counter

Learn more about our mission and the team behind this powerful text analysis tool.

Our Mission

Smart Text Counter was created to provide writers, students, and professionals with a comprehensive text analysis tool that goes beyond simple word counting. We believe that understanding your writing patterns and metrics can significantly improve your writing quality and efficiency.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike basic word counters, our tool provides:

  • Advanced text analytics including keyword density
  • Reading and speaking time estimates
  • Real-time analysis as you type
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Complete privacy - your text never leaves your browser

Our Team

We're a small team of developers and writers passionate about creating useful tools that help people communicate more effectively. Our expertise in natural language processing and web development allows us to create tools that are both powerful and easy to use.

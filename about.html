About WordCounter

WordCounter is a free online tool that helps you count words, characters, sentences, paragraphs and more in real-time. Our goal is to provide writers, students, and professionals with an easy-to-use tool that helps them meet specific word count requirements and improve their writing.

Our Mission

We believe that writing should be accessible to everyone, and that includes having the right tools to make the writing process easier. Our mission is to provide simple, effective tools that help writers of all levels improve their craft.

Features

  • Real-time word and character counting
  • Sentence and paragraph counting
  • Reading level analysis
  • Reading and speaking time estimates
  • Easy text manipulation tools

Our Team

WordCounter is developed by a team of writers and developers who understand the importance of meeting word count requirements and creating well-structured content.

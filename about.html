Our Mission

Smart Text Counter was created with a simple goal: to provide writers, students, professionals, and anyone who works with text a reliable, free, and easy-to-use tool for text analysis.

We believe that understanding your text statistics shouldn't require complex software or expensive subscriptions. That's why we've built a tool that works directly in your browser, respects your privacy, and delivers accurate results instantly.

What We Offer

Our text counter provides comprehensive analysis including:

  • Character Count: Total characters including spaces
  • Word Count: Accurate word counting for any text
  • Sentence Count: Identify sentence boundaries
  • Paragraph Count: Structure analysis

Our Values

Privacy First

Your text never leaves your browser. We don't store, track, or analyze your content on our servers.

Always Free

Accessibility

Our tool works on all devices and browsers, ensuring everyone can access text analysis tools.

The Team

Smart Text Counter is developed and maintained by a small team of developers and writers who understand the importance of text analysis in various fields including content creation, academic writing, and professional communication.

We're constantly working to improve our tool and add new features based on user feedback.

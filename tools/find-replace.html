About Find and Replace

Find and Replace is a powerful text editing feature that allows you to quickly locate specific text and replace it with alternative text throughout your document. This tool is essential for editing, formatting, and cleaning up text efficiently.

Advanced Replacement Options

Case Sensitive

When enabled, the search distinguishes between uppercase and lowercase letters. "Apple" will not match "apple".

Whole Word Only

Matches only complete words. Searching for "cat" will not match "category" or "location".

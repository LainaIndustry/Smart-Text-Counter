About Text Cleaning

Text cleaning is the process of removing formatting issues, extra spaces, line breaks, and other inconsistencies from text. This is particularly useful when working with text copied from PDFs, websites, or other documents that may contain unwanted formatting.

Common Text Issues We Fix

Extra Spaces

Multiple spaces between words or at the beginning/end of lines. Our tool normalizes spacing to single spaces.

Line Breaks

Unwanted line breaks within paragraphs that disrupt text flow. We can remove or normalize these breaks.

Empty Lines

Blank lines between paragraphs or sections. We can remove these to create cleaner, more compact text.

About Case Conversion

Case conversion is the process of changing the capitalization style of text. Different writing contexts require different case styles, and our tool makes it easy to convert between them instantly.

Available Case Styles

Sentence Case

Capitalizes the first letter of the first word in each sentence. All other letters are lowercase unless they are proper nouns.

Lower Case

Converts all text to lowercase letters. Useful for programming variables, email addresses, and certain stylistic choices.

Upper Case

Converts all text to uppercase letters. Often used for headings, acronyms, and emphasis in documents.

Title Case

Capitalizes the first letter of each major word. Commonly used for titles, headings, and book names.
